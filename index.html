Why Hire Me

The short version

I'm an IT student, a graphic designer, a digital artist, an animator, a tutor, and a former supervisor. I've always been drawn to learning things for real rather than staying in one lane, and that habit has shaped a lot of what I can bring to a role.

What I bring

Genuinely multifaceted

IT support, graphic design, digital illustration, animation, social media, SEO, scripting. These aren't directions I'm considering, they're things I've put real time into and actively use.

Creative depth, not just technical breadth

I animate and illustrate in Procreate alongside the design work I do for clients. Having both a creative and technical side has shaped how I approach problems and how I communicate ideas to people who need to understand them.

I teach what I know

I currently tutor people in Python, Bash, and ZSH. Before that, I trained and supervised staff in professional settings. Teaching forces genuine understanding, and it's something I take seriously for that reason.

Self-driven by default

SEO, design tools, scripting, animation. Most of what I know came from learning on my own because I wanted to, not because it was required. That approach tends to stick past the end of any given project.

Reliable when it counts

Nearly a decade of volunteer IT work. Real freelance clients. Real people I've trained and led. I show up, follow through, and take the work seriously whether or not anyone is checking.
