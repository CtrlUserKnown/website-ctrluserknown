Why Hire Me

The short version

I'm an IT student, a graphic designer, a digital artist, an animator, a tutor, and a former supervisor — and that's not a resume padding exercise, that's just who I am. I don't limit myself to what I already know. I move toward whatever interests me and I learn it for real.

What I bring

Genuinely multifaceted

IT support, graphic design, digital illustration, animation, social media, SEO, scripting — these aren't directions I'm considering, they're things I already do. Most people pick a lane. I build across them.

Creative depth, not just technical breadth

I animate and illustrate in Procreate on top of the design work I do for clients. The creative side isn't separate from the tech side — it informs how I approach problems visually and communicate ideas clearly.

I teach what I know

I currently tutor people in Python, Bash, and ZSH. Before that, I trained and supervised staff in professional settings. If I understand something well enough to use it, I can explain it to someone else — that's a signal of real understanding.

Self-driven by default

SEO, design tools, scripting, animation — none of that came from a class. I learn what's useful because I want to, not because it's assigned. That habit doesn't stop when a project ends.

Reliable when it counts

Nearly a decade of volunteer IT work. Real freelance clients. Real people I've trained and led. I show up, I follow through, and I don't need to be watched to do good work.
